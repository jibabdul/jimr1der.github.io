  • Title

  • Artist

  • Year

    • 2018

  • Medium

    • Oil on Canvas 50 x 140cm

Summary

Lefrisha Kensaka's debut at Oriell drew a large spectacle of curators and appreciators from all over the country. They witness the native born Kenyan's debut in awe.

Working with colours and materials only made available to her in her home region of Tana River, she crafted her own oil paint using flowers or fruits to create its own pigment.
